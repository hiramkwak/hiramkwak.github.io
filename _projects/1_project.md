We design and synthesize **halide and sulfide superionic conductors** — the materials at the heart of all-solid-state batteries — and study how their structure governs fast ion transport. Work spans new chemistries (Li/Na halides such as Li<sub>2</sub>ZrCl<sub>6</sub>, Na<sub>2</sub>ZrCl<sub>6</sub>), mechanochemical synthesis, and structure–conductivity relationships.
